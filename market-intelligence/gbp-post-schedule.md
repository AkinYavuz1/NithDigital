## How to Use This

Copy the post copy directly into Google Business Profile (via business.google.com or the Google Maps app). Each post is ~100 words and ready to go. Replace [client name] where noted if you want to name-drop — otherwise leave as written.

Post types:
- **Update** — general news, tips, project highlights
- **Offer** — promotional, includes a specific call to action with a deadline or hook
- **Event** — tied to a local event, season, or date

Suggested posting day: **Tuesday or Wednesday morning**, around 9am. Consistency matters more than the exact day.

## Notes on Tone

- Write like a person, not an agency
- Short sentences. Specific examples. No buzzwords.
- Reference local places — Sanquhar, Dumfries, Annan, Thornhill, Castle Douglas, Moffat, Kirkcudbright, Galloway — wherever it fits naturally
- The CTA at the end of each post should feel like a natural next step, not a hard sell
- Keep hashtags minimal on GBP — unlike Instagram, they add little value here. If you use them: #DumfriesAndGalloway #WebDesign #LocalBusiness
