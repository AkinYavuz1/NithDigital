## 2. Competitive Position

**The local agency landscape in one sentence:** One strong incumbent, a handful of outdated studios, and a cluster of solo freelancers — almost all on WordPress, none with BI capability, most with no public pricing and no Google reviews.

### Where Nith Digital Sits

Nith Digital occupies a structurally uncontested position:

> **The only local agency combining a modern Next.js/React stack, BI and data analytics capability, transparent pricing, and free consultancy tools — built by a qualified Senior BI Developer.**

This is not a marginal differentiation. It is a gap that none of the 20 identified competitors attempts to fill.

| Next.js/React stack | Superior Core Web Vitals, faster load times, scalable architecture | Nobody |
| BI/data analytics capability | Dashboards, conversion tracking, analytics as standard | Nobody |

### Primary Competitor Summary

**Creatomatic (Lockerbie)** — The only real threat. 16 staff, 55 five-star reviews, Scottish Design Awards nominee, 97% retention. However: WordPress-only, no BI capability, no transparent pricing, heavy tourism/heritage focus. Do not compete head-on in self-catering and heritage. Let them own that. Own everything else.

**Eskdale Solutions (Gretna)** — 26 years old, proprietary lock-in CMS, no content marketing, dated website. Not actively recruiting new clients online. Medium-term threat only.

**Digital Social Web (Dumfries)** — The one technically modern solo operator (Nuxt/Vue). Has transparent pricing. Solo capacity = easy to outgrow. Not a serious threat at volume.

**Everyone else** — Sensor Studios (website last updated 2017), Carrick Web Design (broken SSL), Internet Design Shop (still mentions Flash), KA Design/Every Last Pixel (solo, no reviews, no pricing). These are vulnerable, not competitive.
