## Ranking Criteria
Each prospect is scored on four factors (1–10 each), weighted as follows:
- **Need** (40%) — how poor is their digital presence?
- **Ability to Pay** (25%) — are they an established, revenue-generating business?
- **Fit** (20%) — does Nith Digital have a suitable template/service?
- **Accessibility** (15%) — are they local enough for face-to-face?

### Best Cold Call Script Hook by Sector
- **Trades:** "I was searching for a [plumber/electrician] in Dumfries on my phone this morning and your name came up — but your website doesn't work on mobile. I can fix that for under £1,000."
- **B&Bs/Hotels:** "You're paying Booking.com 15% on every booking. I can build you a site that gets direct bookings instead, and it'll pay for itself in a week of saved commission."
- **Salons:** "Your competitor [name competitor] has online booking and you don't. People looking to book at 10pm on a Sunday are going to them, not you. Let me show you what I'd do."
- **Professional services:** "I tried to visit your website and got a security error / couldn't find you on Google. For a firm with [X] years of reputation, that's worth fixing today."

### Recommended Sales Route for Nith Digital
1. Start with trades (volume, fast close, easy product, strong need)
2. Move to B&Bs in Portpatrick, Kirkcudbright, Moffat (tourism season pressure = urgency)
3. Activity operators during winter (plan for summer season)
4. Professional services in Q1/Q2 (post-tax-return period when cash is fresher)
5. Attend DG Chamber of Commerce events — direct access to decision makers across all sectors
6. Approach SOSE and Business Gateway D&G as a recommended digital supplier — this drives inbound leads from their business advisory conversations
