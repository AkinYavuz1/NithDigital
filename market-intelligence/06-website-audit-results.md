## Notable Businesses With NO Website (Facebook Only / Listing Only)
*These represent immediate Nith Digital new-website opportunities*

Based on research across D&G directories, the following types of businesses were consistently found without their own websites:
- Individual B&B operators in Portpatrick, Stranraer, Annan, Gretna
- Small cafes in Newton Stewart, Gatehouse of Fleet, Wigtown
- Sole trader plumbers, electricians and joiners across the region (Sanquhar, Langholm, Lockerbie area)
- Small hair and beauty salons in towns outside Dumfries
- Individual holiday cottage owners across Galloway and Annandale
- Artisan food producers listed on Open Food Network / BigBarn but without own sites
- Small riding schools, guided fishing operators, forest walk guides
