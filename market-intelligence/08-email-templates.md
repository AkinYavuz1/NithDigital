## How to Use These Templates

- Replace all `[bracketed]` text with the specific business details before sending
- Use an email tracking tool (Mailtrack, HubSpot free, or Streak) so you know who opened
- Send Email 1 on day 0, Email 2 on day 5, Email 3 on day 12
- Never send all three to the same contact in the same week
- Subject lines are suggestions — test and adjust based on open rates
