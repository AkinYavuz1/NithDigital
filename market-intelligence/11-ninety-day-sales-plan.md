*Nith Digital | Starting 7 April 2026 | Designed for part-time execution alongside BI contracting*

---

## Overview & Time Budget

Akin is running Nith Digital part-time alongside BI consultancy work. Realistic weekly time available for Nith Digital sales activities: **6–10 hours per week**. This plan is built around that constraint — nothing here requires full-time input. The key is consistency over intensity.

**Phase structure:**
- Weeks 1–2 (7–20 April): Foundation — get Nith Digital visible everywhere it should be
- Weeks 3–4 (21 Apr – 4 May): First outreach batch — top 10 prospects
- Weeks 5–6 (5–18 May): Follow-ups + first networking events
- Weeks 7–8 (19 May – 1 Jun): Second outreach batch + follow up first batch
- Weeks 9–10 (2–15 Jun): Social proof push — case studies and content
- Weeks 11–12 (16–29 Jun): Review, iterate, plan Q3

**90-day revenue target:** 2–3 signed clients. At average project value of £1,000–£1,500, that is £2,000–£4,500. Stretch target: 5 clients.

## CRM Recommendation

**Recommended free tool: Streak (streak.com)**

Why Streak over others:
- Lives inside Gmail — no separate app to log into
- Shows you when a prospect opens your email (open tracking)
- Lets you set follow-up reminders directly from the email thread
- Free tier supports up to 500 contacts — more than enough for Year 1
- Pipeline view shows all prospects and their stage at a glance

**Setup takes about 30 minutes:**
1. Install the Streak Chrome extension
2. Connect to your Gmail
3. Create a Pipeline called "Nith Digital Prospects"
4. Create stages: New Lead → Emailed → Opened → Replied → Proposal Sent → Won → Lost
5. Add each of the 20 hot list prospects as a Box in the pipeline
6. For each Box, record: business name, contact name, phone, email, score, personalised URL, notes from any conversation

**Alternative free options:**
- **HubSpot CRM (free)** — more powerful than Streak, but requires learning curve. Good if you plan to scale. hubspot.com/crm
- **Notion** — if you prefer a database-style CRM. Simple but no email integration.
- **Google Sheets** — perfectly fine if you just need somewhere to track status without fancy features

For the volume in this plan (20–40 prospects in 90 days), Streak or a well-structured Google Sheet is all you need.
