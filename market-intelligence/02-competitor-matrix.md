## Key: Scoring Guide
- **Design Quality** (1-10): Quality of client work/portfolio shown
- **Website Quality** (1-10): Quality of their own website
- **Content/SEO Effort** (1-5): 1=none, 3=moderate, 5=strong content/SEO presence
- **Local Focus** (1-5): 1=national/no local, 3=partial, 5=deeply embedded locally
- **Reviews**: Rating + count where findable
