How much does a website cost?

Sites start at £500 for the build, plus £40 per month for hosting, updates, and support. We'll give you an exact figure after a quick conversation about what you need.

Most sites go live in 2–3 weeks from kickoff. Larger projects with custom features may take a little longer, but we'll agree a timeline upfront.

We handle all updates for you at £35 per hour. No wrestling with a CMS, no accidentally breaking the layout. Just tell us what you need changed and it's done.
