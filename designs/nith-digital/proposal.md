# Nith Digital — Website Redesign Scope

## What we're building

A complete redesign of nithdigital.uk — a dark, animation-rich agency website that demonstrates our capabilities through its own design. Every scroll interaction, every hover state, every transition is proof of what we build for clients.

## Why it matters

The current site is functional but reads as a developer's side project — text-heavy, no images, no scroll animations, flat visual hierarchy. When a prospect Googles "Nith Digital" after a cold call, the site needs to instantly communicate expertise. Right now it doesn't. The local competition in D&G is weak (broken SSL certs, 2017 copyright dates, sites still listing Flash as a service), but we're positioning against Glasgow and Edinburgh agencies — that's where the real benchmark is.

## What's included

- 5 custom design concepts (HTML mockups with full animations)
- Dark mode, animation-rich responsive website
- 5 pages: Home, Work, Services, About, Contact
- Portfolio with video preview thumbnails (MP4s of websites scrolling)
- Scroll-triggered text animations and micro-interactions
- Minimal navigation (logo + CTA visible, hamburger for full menu)
- Contact form with email notifications (Resend)
- SEO: meta tags, JSON-LD schema, sitemap, robots.txt
- Cookie consent and privacy policy (UK GDPR)
- Google Search Console submission
- Existing tool pages remain live and indexed (removed from nav only)

## What we need

- Screen recordings of live projects for video portfolio (OBS captures of sites scrolling)
- Decision on accent colour direction after reviewing 5 design concepts
- Final copy review before build

## Timeline

- Stage 1 (Discovery): Complete

- Stage 3 (Provision): GitHub repo + Vercel project
