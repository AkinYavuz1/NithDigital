## What we'll build

A clean, modern website that showcases your joinery work and makes it easy for customers across Dumfries & Galloway to find and contact you. It'll be designed from scratch — not a template — and built to look good on phones, tablets, and desktops.

## Why it matters

Right now, when someone in Thornhill or Dumfries searches "joiner near me," you don't appear. Your work speaks for itself in person, but none of it is visible online. This site changes that — it puts your work in front of people who are actively looking for a joiner, and gives them a way to get in touch immediately.

## What's included

- Custom-designed website (not a template — designed specifically for Adkin Joinery)
- Works on all devices (phones, tablets, desktops)
- Contact form — enquiries go straight to your email
- Google-optimised so customers can find you when they search for joiners in your area
- Gallery page to showcase your completed projects
- Cookie consent and privacy policy (required by UK law)
- Fully hosted and managed — nothing for you to set up or maintain
- **Google Business profile setup (free)** — so you appear on Google Maps when people search locally

## What I need from you

- 5–10 photos of your best work (phone photos are absolutely fine)
- Your preferred contact details (phone number, email)
- Any specific wording you'd like included (about you, your approach, etc.)
- Your logo if you have one — no problem if you don't

## How it works

1. I'll send you 3 design concepts to choose from
2. You pick the direction you like (or mix elements from different ones)
3. I refine it based on your feedback
4. Once you're happy, the site goes live — usually within a week of approval
