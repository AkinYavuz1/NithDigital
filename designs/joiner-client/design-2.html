How we work

01

Initial conversation

We visit your property, listen to what you need, take measurements, and talk through the options. No obligation, no pressure — just a straightforward chat about your project.

02

Quote and planning

You receive a clear, itemised quote with no hidden costs. Once agreed, we plan the work around your schedule and order materials from trusted suppliers.

03

Build and craft

We get to work. You will see steady progress, a clean site, and regular updates. Every joint, every edge, every finish is done to a standard we are proud to put our name to.

04

Handover

We walk you through the finished work, make sure everything is exactly right, and leave the space spotless. If anything needs adjusting, we sort it there and then.

About

Adkin Joinery is run by Oliver Adkin, a qualified joiner and carpenter based in Closeburn, Thornhill. With four years of trading and a genuine passion for working with wood, Oliver has built a reputation for quality workmanship and reliable service across Dumfries and Galloway.

From full house builds to hand-made kitchens and everyday carpentry, every project is approached with the same care and attention to detail. We keep things straightforward — clear communication, fair pricing, and work we are proud to stand behind.
