About

I’m a Senior BI & Data Professional with over a decade of experience turning complex data into clear, actionable insight. My work spans the NHS, financial services, and energy sectors — building Power BI dashboards, SSRS reports, and data pipelines that teams rely on every day.

I specialise in Power BI semantic models, DAX optimisation, and SQL Server data warehousing. I’ve designed self-service analytics platforms, automated manual reporting workflows (saving 40+ hours a month), and led BI standards across large organisations.

Beyond the day job, I run Nith Digital — a web design and data consultancy based in Dumfries & Galloway, Scotland. I’m also a four-time Commonwealth Karate gold medallist, which taught me more about discipline and preparation than any certification ever could.
