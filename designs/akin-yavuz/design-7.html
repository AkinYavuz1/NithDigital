Who I am

I'm a Senior BI & Data Professional with over a decade of experience turning raw data into dashboards, pipelines, and platforms that organisations actually use to make decisions.

My background spans the NHS, financial services (Openwork, Holloway Friendly), energy (Good Energy), and software consulting.
