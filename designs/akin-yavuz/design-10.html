About

Who I Am

A decade of turning complex data into clear, actionable insight across the UK's most demanding sectors.

I'm a Senior BI & Data Professional with over ten years' experience designing and delivering enterprise-grade reporting solutions. My career spans NHS healthcare analytics, fintech compliance, and energy-sector regulatory reporting — environments where accuracy isn't optional and the data has to work first time.

I specialise in Power BI, SQL Server, and the Microsoft data stack. Whether it's building semantic models that underpin self-service analytics, writing the stored procedures that keep audit trails clean, or migrating an entire organisation's reporting to a new CRM, I focus on work that creates lasting operational value.

Based in Dumfries & Galloway, Scotland, I work remotely and am currently available for new opportunities. Outside of data, I'm a four-time Commonwealth gold medallist in powerlifting — the same discipline and consistency I bring to every project.
