About

Turning data into
decisions since 2016

I specialise in the Microsoft data stack — Power BI, SQL Server, Azure, SSRS — building reporting platforms that scale across large organisations. Over a decade, I have worked in NHS healthcare analytics, financial services compliance, energy sector regulation, and agile consulting.

My approach focuses on self-service BI: designing semantic models, datasets, and governance frameworks that let non-technical teams build their own reports independently. I believe the best BI solution is the one people actually use.

I hold Microsoft certifications in Power BI and SQL, a Certified Scrum Master qualification, and a Diploma in Leadership. Outside of data, I am a four-time Commonwealth Games gold medallist in karate — discipline and precision define everything I do.
