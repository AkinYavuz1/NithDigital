# Connecting Your Facebook & Instagram to Nith Digital
**A step-by-step guide for business owners**

This guide walks you through connecting your Facebook Page and Instagram account so Nith Digital can schedule and publish posts on your behalf. The whole process takes about 15 minutes and you only need to do it once.

---

## Before you start — checklist

Make sure you have all four of these in place before following the steps below:

- [ ] **A Facebook Business Page** (not just a personal profile — most businesses already have one)
- [ ] **An Instagram Business account** (see Step 1 below if you're not sure)
- [ ] **Your Instagram linked to your Facebook Page** (see Step 2 below)
- [ ] **Admin access to your Facebook Page** (you must be the Page Admin, not just an Editor)

---

## Step 1 — Switch Instagram to a Business account

If your Instagram account is already a Business or Creator account, skip to Step 2.

1. Open the Instagram app on your phone
2. Tap your profile picture (bottom right) to go to your profile
3. Tap the three lines in the top right corner
4. Tap **Settings and privacy**
5. Tap **Account type and tools**
6. Tap **Switch to Professional Account**
7. Choose **Business** when prompted
8. Follow the on-screen steps — you can skip the optional parts

Your account is now a Business account. This is free and doesn't affect your followers or posts.

---

## Step 2 — Link your Instagram to your Facebook Page

This is the most important step. Without it, we can't post to Instagram on your behalf.

1. Open the Instagram app
2. Go to your profile and tap the three lines (top right)
3. Tap **Settings and privacy**
4. Tap **Account type and tools**
5. Tap **Linked accounts** (or **Connected accounts**)
6. Tap **Facebook**
7. Log in to Facebook when prompted
8. Select your **Business Page** from the list (not your personal profile)
9. Tap **Connect**

You should see a confirmation that your Instagram is now connected to your Facebook Page.

> **Not seeing your Page in the list?** You may not be the Admin on the Page. Ask whoever manages the Page to make you an Admin, or contact us and we'll help.

---

## Step 3 — Find your Facebook Page ID

Your Page ID is a number that identifies your Facebook Page. Here's how to find it:

1. Go to **facebook.com** on a computer (not the mobile app)
2. Navigate to your Business Page
3. Click **About** in the left-hand menu
4. Scroll down to the bottom of the About section
5. Look for **Page ID** — it will be a long number, e.g. `134895793791914`

Write this number down or copy it — you'll need to share it with us.

> **Can't find it?** Try clicking the three dots (...) near the top of your Page, then click **About this page** — the Page ID is listed there.

---

## Step 4 — Share your details with us

Once you've completed Steps 1–3, send us the following:

1. **Your Facebook Page ID** (the number from Step 3)
2. **The email address on your Facebook account** (so we can add you to our system)

That's all we need from you. We'll take care of the rest — generating the access tokens and getting everything connected — during a short screen share or phone call.

> **What happens next:** We'll send you a Facebook notification asking you to approve our access. It takes about 5 minutes and we can do it together over a call. You'll see exactly what permissions you're granting before you approve anything.

---

## What permissions we'll ask for

When we connect your accounts, Facebook will show you a list of permissions. Here's what each one means:

| Permission | What it lets us do |
|---|---|
| See your Pages | Shows us which Pages you manage so we can pick the right one |
| Manage your Page posts | Lets us create and schedule posts on your behalf |
| Read Page engagement | Lets us see how posts are performing so we can report back to you |
| Basic Instagram access | Lets us connect to your Instagram account |
| Publish to Instagram | Lets us post photos and videos to Instagram on your behalf |

We will never use these permissions to read your private messages, run ads, or make any changes to your Page without your knowledge.

---

## Important things to know

**You stay in control.** You can remove our access at any time by going to your Facebook settings and removing the Nith Digital app from your connected apps.

**Changing your Facebook password.** If you change your Facebook password, it will disconnect our access and we'll need to reconnect. Just let us know.

**Image and video content.** When we post on your behalf, we'll either use content you supply to us or content we create for you as part of your social media package. We will always confirm the content plan with you before anything goes live.

**Instagram story posting.** Instagram Stories cannot currently be posted via the API — this is a Meta restriction, not something we can work around. Stories would need to be posted manually from the Instagram app.

**Post limits.** Instagram allows a maximum of 100 scheduled posts per day per account. For a standard posting schedule (3 posts per week) this is nowhere near the limit.
